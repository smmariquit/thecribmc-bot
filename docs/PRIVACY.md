# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 4, 2026

This Discord bot supports The Crib Minecraft server community. This policy describes how the bot handles information when you interact with it.

## What we process

- **Discord account data** Discord provides when you use slash commands (e.g. user ID, username, guild context) — used only to respond to commands.
- **Interaction content** only when required for a documented bot feature — not used for advertising or resale.

## What we do not do

- We do **not** sell personal data.
- We do **not** use your data for third-party advertising.
- We do **not** share data except as needed to operate the bot (e.g. hosting) or when required by law.
